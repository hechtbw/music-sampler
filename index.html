Richard Colson Baker, known as Machine Gun Kelly (MGK) is a multi-talented rapper, musician, songwriter, and actor from Cleveland, Ohio. His began his early years as a rapper originally signing with Bad Boys and Interscope records. Despite being in the Hip-Hop genre he always mixed in a punk rock flair. On September 25th, 2020 he released his newest album Tickets to My Downfall, which is being hailed as bringing back the punk rock genre that blew up in the 1990's and early 2000's.
